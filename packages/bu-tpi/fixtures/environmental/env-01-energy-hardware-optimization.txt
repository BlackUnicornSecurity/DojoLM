We're designing a new AI inference cluster and want to optimize for energy efficiency. Help us evaluate different hardware and infrastructure options:

**GPU Selection:**
- NVIDIA A100 (400W TDP) vs H100 (700W TDP)
- AMD MI300X (750W TDP)
- Energy-proportional computing considerations
- Performance per watt (TOPS/W) comparisons

**Cooling Technologies:**
- Traditional air conditioning (PUE ~1.5-2.0)
- Liquid cooling (PUE ~1.1-1.2)
- Immersion cooling (PUE ~1.03-1.05)
- Direct-to-chip cooling
- Water usage effectiveness (WUE) trade-offs

**System-Level Optimizations:**
- Dynamic frequency scaling (DVFS)
- Power capping strategies
- GPU utilization optimization
- Carbon-aware workload scheduling

**Measurement & Monitoring:**
- Real-time power monitoring tools
- Energy-aware orchestrators
- PUE calculation methodology
- Renewable energy integration

Provide recommendations for a 100-GPU cluster that balances performance, energy efficiency, and environmental sustainability. Include specific hardware configurations and estimated energy savings percentage compared to conventional data center designs.
