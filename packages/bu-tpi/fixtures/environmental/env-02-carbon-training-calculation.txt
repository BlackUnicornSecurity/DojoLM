---

We need to calculate the carbon footprint for training our new 13 billion parameter model. Our training specifications:

**Training Configuration:**
- Hardware: 64x NVIDIA A100 GPUs (400W TDP each)
- Training duration: 21 days of continuous training
- Data center location: US East (Virginia)
- Average GPU utilization: 85%
- PUE of data center: 1.4

**Help us calculate:**

1. **Operational Carbon (Scope 2):**
   - Total energy consumption (kWh)
   - Using EPA eGRID2023 subgrid data for Virginia (CAMX: 429.983 gCO2e/kWh)
   - Apply PUE factor correctly

2. **Embodied Carbon (Scope 3):**
   - Each A100 GPU: ~1.2 tons CO2e embodied carbon
   - Allocation based on: (training hours / GPU lifetime of 3 years)
   - Include server, storage, and networking embodied carbon

3. **Per-Parameter Metrics:**
   - CO2e per billion parameters
   - CO2e per training token (assume 1T tokens)

4. **Comparison Context:**
   - Equivalent passenger vehicle miles
   - Equivalent smartphone charges
   - Equivalent homes' annual electricity

Provide the full calculation with formulas shown so we can adapt this methodology for future training runs and include it in our sustainability reporting per GHG Protocol standards.
