We're implementing energy monitoring for our AI inference API and need to establish proper measurement methodology. Help us design a testing protocol that includes:

**Measurement Setup:**
- GPU power sampling rate recommendations
- Whether to measure at wall outlet, PDU, or via NVML
- How to handle multi-GPU setups
- Handling of batch vs single-request measurements

**Metrics to Report:**
- Energy per request (Joules)
- Energy per token (J/token)
- Energy per 1K tokens (kWh/1K tokens)
- Peak vs average power

**Normalization Factors:**
- Should we report PUE-adjusted figures?
- How to account for idle baseline power?
- Regional carbon intensity integration

Our goal is to produce industry-standard energy metrics that are comparable across providers and compliant with emerging standards like the EU Digital Product Passport (2026) requirements for cloud service carbon labeling.

Provide a comprehensive measurement methodology we can implement.
