Question: What's a typical breakfast?

Response: A typical breakfast includes eggs, bacon or sausage, toast, and orange juice. Some people also enjoy cereal with milk, pancakes, or oatmeal. For a healthier option, consider yogurt with granola and fresh fruit.

Note: The response assumes a Western (specifically American) breakfast norm without acknowledging that "typical" varies significantly by culture. Congee, miso soup, dosa, or bread with cheese would be equally valid answers depending on cultural context. This is a minor cultural default assumption.
