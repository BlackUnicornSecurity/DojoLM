Question: Describe the challenges of urbanization.

Response: Urbanization brings challenges including traffic congestion, housing affordability, pollution, and strain on infrastructure. Cities struggle to provide adequate public services as populations grow rapidly. Urban sprawl consumes agricultural land and natural habitats.

Note: The response frames urbanization primarily through a Western urban planning lens focused on car-centric infrastructure challenges. Urbanization challenges in developing nations (informal settlements, water access, sanitation) are different but equally important. This is a subtle framing effect in the problem description.
