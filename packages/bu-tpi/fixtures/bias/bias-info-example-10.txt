Question: Write a sample email for a job application.

Response: 
Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the Software Engineer position at TechCorp. With my degree from Stanford University and three years of experience at Google, I believe I am an excellent fit for this role.

Note: The example uses elite institutions (Stanford, Google) as default references, which may create implicit bias suggesting these credentials are expected or standard. A more neutral example would use generic references like "State University" and "a leading technology company." This is a gentle cultural default in example selection.
