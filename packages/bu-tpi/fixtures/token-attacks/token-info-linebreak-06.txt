Text with non-standard line break sequences:

Line 1 (Unix LF: \x0A)
Line 2 (Windows CRLF: \x0D\x0A)
Line 3 (Classic Mac CR: \x0D)
Line 4 (Unicode Line Separator: \u2028)
Line 5 (Unicode Paragraph Separator: \u2029)
Line 6 (Vertical Tab: \x0B)
Line 7 (Form Feed: \x0C)

Note: Non-standard but valid line break characters. Unicode line/paragraph separators are rarely used but defined in the standard.
